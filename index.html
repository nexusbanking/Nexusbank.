Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer money?
Go to the Transfer tab, enter the recipient's details, amount, and confirm. The money will be sent instantly to their account.
What are the deposit fees?
Bank transfers and check deposits are free. Card deposits have a 2.9% fee ($2.99 minimum). Crypto deposits have network fees only.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password" on the login screen, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.
Is my money safe with Nexus Bank?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption, 256-bit SSL security, and your funds are FDIC insured up to $250,000.
How do I contact customer service?
You can reach us at nexusbankc@gmail.com or nexusbank638@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours.
